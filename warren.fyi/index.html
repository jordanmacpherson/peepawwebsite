YSU Civic Engagement Survey
This is an effort to encourage Civic engagement and getting people involved in their community and government. In this study, you will be asked to share your thoughts about the current needs and desires of Warren Voters.
Warren Civic Engagement Survey
This survey was created by faculty from Youngstown State University (Political Science Dept). We are conducting a study to investigate the current political climate of the citizens of the City of Warren.
